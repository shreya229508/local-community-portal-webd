Senior Center Assistant

Make a difference in the lives of seniors by helping organize and facilitate activities at our local senior center. Your presence and support can brighten someone's day!

Requirements:

  • Minimum age: 18 years
  • Good communication skills
  • Patience and empathy
  • Basic first aid knowledge (training provided)

Schedule: Flexible shifts available Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM

Food Bank Helper

Join our team at the local food bank to help sort, organize, and distribute food to those in need. Your contribution will help ensure no one in our community goes hungry.

Requirements:

  • Minimum age: 16 years
  • Ability to lift 25 pounds
  • Reliable and punctual
  • Team player attitude

Schedule: Tuesday-Saturday, Morning (8 AM - 12 PM) or Afternoon (1 PM - 5 PM) shifts
